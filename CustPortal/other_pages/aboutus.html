About Us
What is HealYos

HealYos is a mobile enabled Physiotherapy and Fitness startup with a team of highly qualified, vetted and trained therapists. Our therapists are available to you at the push of a button and will come to your home or office at your convenience, a far better alternative to looking for parking and waiting for hours in a clinic.

Our Story

Like many people of our generation we suffered from back pain, knee problems and other such lifestyle related injuries but neglected them since great service was never convenient. We started building this service for our friends and family because, we believe that, like you, they deserve the best. Our founders Salil Bhargava and Shweta Mani combined their expertise and passion to create mobile enabled expert healing services that simplify the experience and deliver the best possible care to you.

On this journey, we’re backed by Sancheti Institute, South Asia’s leading Orthopaedic Institution and Healthcare Academy.
