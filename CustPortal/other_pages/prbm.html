Pune Running Beyond Myself 2017

Healyos - Official Recovery Partner

Pune Running Beyond Myself is organised by Pune Running Sports Foundation, the largest running community in Pune, dedicated to the cause of promoting health and fitness. We motivate individuals to transform themselves by adopting a healthier lifestyle and experience the countless benefits of running. This year's edition will see more than 12500 runners across 5 different categories of 3km, 5km, 10km, 21km and 42km.

Healyos is proud to announce that it will be the official recovery partner for PRBM 2017. We will be at the recovery tent at the start/finish line and along the route at enroute recovery tents or on bikes.

We have a wide array of recovery methods planned for all the runners both post run and pre run at the Expo. We are geared for the event with more than 60 physiotherapists thousands of icepacks hundreds of sprays as well as firstaid. So please feel free to come to us for all your physiotherapy needs.
