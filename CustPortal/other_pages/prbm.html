Pune Running Beyond Myself 2017

Healyos - Official Recovery Partner

In a first of its kind recovery zone seen at a marathon in Pune, HealYos one of India’s leading physiotherapy and wellness companies successfully treated over 2000 runners as part of the Pune Running Beyond Myself marathon, conducted at Balewadi Stadium on Nov 19,2017.

As official recovery partners for PRBM the HealYos recovery zone had over 90 trained physiotherapists on hand catering to the needs of over 12000 runners who participated in the event. As the runners crossed the finish line the Healyos team was available to treat all running related injuries and aid in their post run recovery.

Pune Running Beyond Myself - a run in its 6th year is organised by Pune Running - a group of passionate runners and volunteers. On Sunday, 19-November thousands of Punekars #Outran themselves through the city of Pune. Over 12000 participants from all walk of life ran in five different categories 3 km, 5 km, 10 km, 21 km and 42 km. The list of runners also includes non-sighted and differently abled runners.

"PRBM17 was one of the biggest events in Pune city with over 12000 runners participating. HEALYOS, the event's official recovery partner did exemplary work attending to thousands of runners with their post run stretches and recovery. Their team of tens of trained physios also provided enroute service to the runners. HEALYOS's performance was one of the highlights of PRBM2017." said Avinash Deshmukh, core organising committee member of PRBM 2017.

“As the running community in India expands, marathoners will expect world class recovery and physiotherapy post run. It is our endeavor to bring these best in class treatment programs to runners not just in Pune but across all major cities in India over the next few years” said Salil Bhargava CEO and Founder, Healyos.
